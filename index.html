Spiritual Significance

In the tradition of Islamic gatherings, we commence our ceremony with a prayer to seek Allah's blessings and guidance. This prayer sets the spiritual tone for the event and reminds us of the sacred nature of knowledge.

The opening prayer will be led by Imam Yusuf Abdulrahman, who has been serving as the spiritual guide for our academy for the past five years. His deep connection with the Quran and Sunnah makes him the perfect choice to inaugurate this blessed event.

Academy Introduction

مقدمة عن الأكاديمية
An overview of Al-Hanafi International Academy's mission, vision, and achievements.
10:15 AM - 10:25 AM

Founded in 2020, Al-Hanafi International Academy has grown to become a leading institution in Islamic education. Our mission is to provide authentic Islamic knowledge while integrating contemporary educational methodologies.

Our Achievements

  • Over 500 students graduated since inception
  • 98% student satisfaction rate
  • Recipient of the 2024 Excellence in Islamic Education Award
  • Partnerships with 15 international Islamic institutions

Our Vision

To cultivate a generation of scholars who are grounded in authentic Islamic tradition while being equipped to address contemporary challenges.

Welcome Address

كلمة الترحيب
Formal welcome address by the Academy Director, Ustadh Basheer Hanafiy.
10:35 AM - 10:50 AM

Ustadh Basheer Hanafiy

Director, Al-Hanafi International Academy

With over 15 years of experience in Islamic education, Ustadh Basheer has been instrumental in shaping the academy's vision and curriculum. A graduate of the Islamic University of Madinah, he specializes in Fiqh and Islamic Jurisprudence.

Under his leadership, the academy has grown from a small study group to an internationally recognized institution with students from over 20 countries. His dedication to authentic Islamic knowledge combined with modern teaching methodologies has transformed the lives of countless students.

Director's Message Excerpt

"Today marks a significant milestone in the journey of our graduates. As they stand at the threshold of a new chapter, I am reminded of the prophetic tradition that emphasizes the pursuit of knowledge from cradle to grave. Our graduates have shown exceptional dedication in their studies, and today we celebrate not just their academic achievements, but their spiritual growth and commitment to serving the Ummah."

"The challenges facing our Ummah today require scholars who are deeply rooted in authentic Islamic tradition while being able to address contemporary issues. I am confident that our graduates possess this balance and will be beacons of light in their communities."

- Ustadh Basheer Hanafiy

Keynote Lecture 1

CHALLENGES FACED BY STUDENTS OF KNOWLEDGE AFTER GRADUATION

المواجهات التي ينبغيها طلبة العلم بعد التخزين أسباب وحلول

Shaykh Rasheed Salahudeen Omotoso

BSc Computer Science, BEd Islamic Studies
Shaykh Rasheed is a renowned Islamic scholar and educator with a unique background in both Islamic and secular education. After completing his degree in Computer Science at the University of Ibadan, he pursued Islamic Studies at Majmaa University. This dual perspective allows him to address contemporary challenges faced by graduates in the modern world.

With over 12 years of teaching experience, Shaykh Rasheed has developed specialized programs to help graduates transition into their roles as community leaders and educators. His research focuses on the integration of traditional Islamic knowledge with contemporary educational methodologies.

THE BENEFITS AND VIRTUES OF SEEKING KNOWLEDGE

طلب العلم فوائد وفضائله

Dr. Muhammad Musa Babalola

Director, University Institute of Education and Culture
Dr. Babalola is a distinguished scholar with over 20 years of experience in Islamic education. As the director of the University Institute of Education and Culture, he has pioneered innovative approaches to Islamic pedagogy. A graduate of Al-Azhar University, he holds a PhD in Islamic Sciences with a specialization in Educational Methodology.

His groundbreaking work on the revival of Islamic learning traditions has been published in numerous academic journals. Dr. Babalola is particularly known for his ability to connect classical Islamic scholarship with modern educational theory, making him a sought-after speaker at international conferences.
